more about me

ELLO! ember sācher's my penname, i am a hobbyist OC artist and a wannabe comic creator! strictly self-indulgent and increasingly fanatical, i need to make a pretty picture every day or i will die.

i have a fondness for sea & space-inspired characters, themes of cycles and endings, and polite men and insane women! most of the art on this site is dedicated to characters from a space fantasy of mine, PTC, whose story i scatter here in small discombobulated increments.

you may peek at more of my interests and inspos in the "favorites" tab, look at the OCs in question on their dedicated page, and find extra updates on the site profile :)

where does the name "ember sācher" come from, anyhow? (a ramble on pronunciations and inspirations)

fun trivia if anyone rly wants to know--!

my penname is inspired by the band, “alice schach and the magic orchestra!” i rather impulsively decided i wanted to pay tribute to them somehow :} thus became schach —> sācher overtime. normally, "sacher" is not meant to have the hard ā over it, but i had the inclination to pronounce it like "say-sure." germans may feel free to beat me to death.
