closing thoughts

mf. i was in and out of artblock these entire 2 months lol. after coming from the high that was returning from uni in may and making 3264868137423 rendered pieces, i was completely tuckered out by the time june rolled around. i swear all of the technique i learned in april-may became unlearned here LOL i still have no idea what i'm doing.

i did more lined/cell-shaded pieces in june-july, but in exchange, i entirely forgot how to paint. rip. i found that i loveeee the process of lineart a lot, but integrating it into the scribbly, messy, painterly style i like is actually so difficult lol. maybe i'm not experimenting enough (i still am having trouble allowing my lines to be imperfect) but man. MAN i do not have the patience like i used to for that ;o;
