zo doodledump

creation date: june 5


character featured: enzo

i don’t usually do page spreads with multiple figures on the canvas, so ig in that way, this artwork was a little out of my comfort zone? Makes sense tho, because i think this piece was one of the more “mid” works of june-july. I think i could have filled up the page more, but it’s whatever. i don’t remember which brush i used, but i recall it being real nice to work with, so it wasn’t all a waste. i had fun with it, i was giddy with it.

i mostly worked on these in the late hours of the night when i couldn't sleep, nothing too artistically significant about them. along w some general boredom relief, i was also trying to figure out what clothes enzo could wear under his uniform and how they'd work w his hugeass tail. it did its job, but i eventually gotta make a reference sheet for this fit. Noooo.........
